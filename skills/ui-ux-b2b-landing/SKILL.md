# UI/UX Skill for B2B Landing and Service Pages

## When to use
Use this skill when:
- building marketing pages
- writing or structuring hero sections
- arranging blocks on service pages
- deciding CTA placement and hierarchy
- simplifying messaging for conversion

## Core UX goal
The primary conversion goal is:
**Записаться на консультацию**

Every key page should support that action.

## Messaging principles
- One screen = one clear idea
- Headline addresses the client’s pain
- Subheadline explains the business value
- CTA is visible and unambiguous
- Prefer clarity over cleverness

## User context
The audience includes:
- business owners
- directors
- managers of SMB companies

Their pain points include:
- lead loss
- chaos in WhatsApp and calls
- lack of visibility
- weak employee control
- no reports

## Layout principles
- Start with a strong hero
- Then confirm the pain
- Then explain the solution
- Then show services / proof / cases
- Then repeat CTA

## CTA rules
Primary CTA:
**Записаться на консультацию**

Use it:
- in the hero
- after important trust sections
- near the bottom of key pages

## Content rules
- Avoid generic claims
- Avoid long dense paragraphs
- Use short sections with clear structure
- Make benefits concrete and business-oriented

## Trust-building
Prioritize:
- response time
- years of experience
- number of companies served
- projects completed
- work under a turnkey model

## Anti-patterns
- Do not overload the page with decorative content
- Do not use abstract slogans without substance
- Do not add multiple competing CTAs
- Do not make the interface feel noisy or chaotic
